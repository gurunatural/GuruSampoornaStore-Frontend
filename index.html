Our Journey of Purity

1. Premium Selection

Only the finest, high-quality seeds are selected to ensure the best starting point for our oils.

2. Gentle Extraction

The selected seeds are gently pressed in a traditional wooden Ghani, a slow process that preserves all the natural nutrients.

3. Hygienic Bottling

The pure oil is hygienically packed into bottles, sealing in the freshness and goodness for you to enjoy.

4. Delivery to Customer

Our freshly prepared products are carefully packaged and swiftly delivered right to your doorstep.

Co-Founder

Guru Sampoorna was founded in 2017 by visionary entrepreneur Madhav Patidar, inspired by the teachings and ideals of Shri Rajiv Ji Dixit. The company was established with the mission of offering high-quality, swadeshi chemical products across India, supporting the growth of the Swadeshi movement and empowering local farmers.

Madhav’s commitment to the movement is reflected in every aspect of the business, aiming to deliver products that are both beneficial for consumers and the country. Co-founder Devvrat plays a crucial role in overseeing the company's quality control measures, ensuring the highest standards of hygiene and consistency throughout the production process. Meanwhile, co-founder Govind is dedicated to providing an exceptional customer experience, addressing queries and ensuring that Guru Sampoorna's products are accessible to a wide audience.

Together, the team is driving Guru Sampoorna toward a future where quality, sustainability, and community development go hand in hand.
